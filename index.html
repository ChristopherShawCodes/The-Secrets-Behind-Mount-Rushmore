Mount Rushmore

It's one of the most recognizable, and most visited landmarks in America. But Mount Rushmore has a secret. The sculpture, which famously depicts four former US Presidents as 60 foot granite faces, was constructed between 1927 and 1941 under the direction of architect Gutzon Borglum. Everybody knows that. But few people know about the secret project Borglum was working on as part of the monument.
